About Us

Strong Roots, Unlimited growth

Pillars of hope children’s home is an organisation that is managed by Vitallis Nyambeka and his wife Glady’s Muhonja. The primary purpose of our home is to provide a habitable environment for the growth of the vulnerable children, orphaned and those who have lost their families; providing basic needs, love, education and skills to empower them. We been in operation for more than five years and continue to serve the community as best as we can.
