Pillars of Hope Gallery

We the family of Pillars of Hope children’s home take the pleasure to introduce our ministry to you. We are a society registered under the registrar of societies to offer a home and support to vulnerable children in Kenya. Pillars of Hope children’s home has been in operation for more than five years and over the years we have been able to encounter successes and challenges in equal measures. We take this opportunity to invite you to find time and visit with us at the home in Athi River. We recently acquired a piece of land on agreement with one of our well-wishers and are in the process of setting up a home. It is on this ground that we wish for support and encouragement to be able to meet the needs of these children whom we are not fully able to support on our own. We kindly request that you to visit and assess our needs for yourselves and see if there is a way we can work together on how to help these needy children. Thank you and we do look forward to your visit.
